Detailed Guidance for Your Career Choices

What to do after 10th? ▾

  • Select the right stream: Science, Commerce, or Arts based on your interest.
  • Choose a good junior college or high school.
  • Consider preparing for entrance exams if planning for engineering, medicine, or other professional courses.

What to do after 12th? ▾

  • Pick a degree course aligned with your career goals (e.g., B.Tech, B.Sc, B.Com).
  • Research top colleges in Maharashtra or India with competitive cutoffs.
  • Prepare for entrance exams like JEE, NEET, or CLAT, if applicable.
